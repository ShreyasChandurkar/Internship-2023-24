About the Founder

Suresh Gulage

Founder, Mangal Agro Farm's & Chaitanya Lagdi Ghana and Organics

Hailing from Maka village in Ahmednagar's Newasa Taluka, this individual was immersed in agriculture from childhood, fostering an enduring affection for farming. Growing up amidst farming communities ignited a profound passion, driving a vision to steer farmers away from conventional fertilizers towards organic farming methods. Envisioning a sustainable agricultural landscape, he aimed to advocate eco-conscious practices, prioritizing environmental sustainability in crop cultivation. His mission centered on urging farmers to adopt organic methodologies, fostering a paradigm shift towards more environmentally friendly and sustainable farming practices in the region.

Founder's Journey Timeline

Starting Point

In May 2016, Suresh Gulage embarked on a pioneering venture by launching Mangal Agro Farm, a YouTube channel dedicated to promoting organic farming practices. His vision was to disseminate valuable information and expertise in agriculture, catering to a broad audience eager to embrace sustainable farming methodologies. Leveraging the digital platform, the channel served as an educational hub, offering insights into various facets of agricultural cultivation. Mangal Agro Farm became a reservoir of knowledge, empowering viewers with diverse techniques, from soil enrichment methods to crop rotation strategies. Suresh Gulage's commitment to organic farming resonated strongly with audiences seeking environmentally conscious and healthier agricultural approaches. The channel's content not only educated but also inspired individuals to adopt eco-friendly practices in their agricultural pursuits. As the channel's popularity soared, it garnered a significant following, culminating in the prestigious achievement of the Silver Play Button, symbolizing its reach exceeding 100,000 subscribers. Gulage's initiative transformed into a pivotal platform driving awareness and advocacy for sustainable agriculture.

Milestone Reached

By 2022, Mangal Agro Farm, under the guidance of Mr. Suresh Gulage, attained a significant milestone, amassing a substantial base of over 100,000 YouTube subscribers. This remarkable achievement was recognized with the prestigious Silver Play Button from the platform, acknowledging the channel's impactful content on organic farming practices and agricultural insights. Following this success, Gulage propelled Mangal Agro Farm into a new phase by introducing Chaitanya Lakadi Ghana and Organics. This innovative venture aimed to provide Lakadi Ghana machines designed specifically for extracting natural oils from various sources, notably peanuts. The shift in focus within Mangal Agro Farm aligned with the growing demand for sustainable and organic methods in oil extraction. This expansion not only diversified the channel's offerings but also catered to a market seeking eco-friendly, efficient ways to procure natural oils. Gulage's initiative within Mangal Agro Farm reflected a commitment to meeting evolving consumer needs while promoting environmentally conscious agricultural practices.

Current Endeavors

As of now, Mangal Agro Farm has unveiled its latest venture, Chaitanya Flour Mill, introduced on October 24, 2023. This specialized establishment focuses on the production of diverse grain flours, including wheat and jowar varieties. However, the services offered by Chaitanya Flour Mill are currently limited exclusively to the local village area. This new addition to the Mangal Agro Farm's repertoire represents a strategic expansion into grain processing, catering to the local community's dietary needs. By offering various types of grain flours, the venture aims to provide wholesome and locally sourced products, potentially enhancing the availability of diverse, nutritionally rich flours within the village vicinity. While presently confined to the village area, the establishment of Chaitanya Flour Mill signifies a promising step towards fulfilling the demand for quality grain products and fostering a stronger local agricultural economy. The localized approach demonstrates a commitment to serving the immediate community while potentially paving the way for broader outreach in the future.

Future Goals

Mangal Agro Farm maintains a resilient vision anchored in sustainability, community involvement, and continual expansion. Their commitment revolves around advocating and exemplifying eco-friendly farming practices through their platform. By encouraging viewers to adopt sustainable methods, the initiative actively contributes to environmental conservation, promoting soil health, reduced chemical usage, and biodiversity preservation. Community engagement lies at the heart of this endeavor, fostering a collective dedication to sustainable living. Through dialogue and education, Mangal Agro Farm cultivates a network of individuals passionate about eco-friendly farming, amplifying their impact beyond digital boundaries. The enduring goal for ongoing expansion signifies not only growth but also a wider dissemination of sustainable practices, aiming to reach and influence a more extensive audience. With an unwavering focus on sustainability, Mangal Agro Farm persists in championing eco-consciousness, engaging communities, and nurturing a greener agricultural landscape for a better, more environmentally friendly future.
