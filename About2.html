Our Mission

Mangal Agro Farm is dedicated to empowering agricultural enthusiasts by providing educational support and essential resources for diverse ventures. Their mission revolves around fostering knowledge and aiding individuals in the agricultural domain. On the other hand, Chaitanya Lakadi Ghana endeavors to revolutionize organic oil extraction. Their focus lies in pioneering this sector through cutting-edge machinery, comprehensive training programs, and advocating for sustainable practices. Their aim is to not just provide equipment but to cultivate expertise, ensuring a shift towards sustainable and innovative approaches within the organic oil extraction industry.
